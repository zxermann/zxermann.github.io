About

Our Organization greatly believes that every woman has the faculty to do the unimaginable if given the right platform.

Our Story

The Miss Universe Anambra Organisation inspires young women to realise their personal best, as we constantly aim at adroitly discovering, perfectly harnessing and optimally developing their innate qualities. We believe every woman has got very unique potentials and if given a platform, would do stunning exploits as well as make amazing differences.

Having provided the best platform, we bield every young woman out there to step out, be smart, be her best self and always be confident because she is a Beauty Extraordinaire.

Our Mission

Inspiring Women:
One major hurdle for women to surmount is lack of self-confidence. With this stereotype in mind, we strive to break it in our contestants by inspiring them to be very confident. Though we are a competition, we also inspire them to see friends in one another and create a strong network of associates on which to draw help and support whenever need be. Providing Opportunities One of our solemn duties is to ensure that job and career opportunities are provided for not just Winners or Titleholders but also every contestant who goes through the Miss Universe Anambra system. Bettering lives

Our Organization prioritises the importance of giving back to society and so creates a dedicated platform for charity. Our Winners would be obliged to better the lives of others by devoting themselves to raising awareness and the much needed funds for philanthropy.

Our Vision

As a registered pageant company, the Miss Universe Anambra Organisation aspires to stand out uniquely and be one of the best pageant brands in the country and, in the nearest future, the world at large. This we aim to accomplish by continually helping not just Winners but every contestant who goes through the Miss Universe Anambra system achieve their personal life goals, be role models who advocate for humane issues, be voices that influence as well as initiate positive change and above all, assist them attain high profile careers in government, politics, sports, movies, broadcasting plus a plethora others to name a few.
