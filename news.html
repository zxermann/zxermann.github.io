REGISTRATION DATE

January 12, 2019 by Admin

Registration for Miss Universe Anambra 2019 would commence on the 10th of February, 2019.
Potential contestants can apply to enter the competition online via the Organisation's official website only as forms won't be made available either physically or on any other platform.

REGISTRATION Registration for Miss Universe Anambra 2019 has commenced. Follow steps below to register:

1. Click the "Register" button.
2. Fill in the online form correctly.
3. Click the "Submit" button.
4. Check SMS or mail for contestant ID.
5. Present it at the casting venue for validation.
*Note: Steps 4 and 5 are for successfully shortlisted contestants only.
