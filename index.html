7 important things you need know about MISS UNIVERSE ANAMBRA 2019

1. Registration is totally free: No form purchase, no entry fee payment whatsoever.
2. No Camp fee payment: Meals, Resort/Hotel plus all Logistics for the camping exercise would be fully sponsored and paid for, no fee is required of you.
3. Open to every lady of Igbo origin (not just Anambra Origins), but who must be at least 18 and under 28 years of age before January 1st in the year she wishes to contest.
4. No need for a break-the-bank budget or Sponsor(s) to contest or win, just abide by all pageant rules, follow instructions to the letter and you're good to win.
5. Influencial persons, team members, a judge or even all judges can't help you win because the crowd don't lie, only your performance can, don't be beguiled.
6. Car, cash and other prizes belong permanently to Winner, and on no condition would it be handed over alongside Crown.
7. To enter contest: Visit www.missuniverseanambra.com, click the 'Register Now' button, fill in details, submit and if successfully shortlisted, you get a confirmation SMS.
