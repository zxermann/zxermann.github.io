*COMPETITION RULES*

To be Miss Universe Anambra, you must be smart, inwardly beautiful and above all, confident enough to convince the Organisation, teeming audience and Judges to believe in you, your ambitions, aspirations and goals. Outward beauty alone doesn't take youÂ far in the competition as we believe every woman is beautiful in her own unique way.

The women who compete must embody our strapline, BEAUTY EXTRAORDINAIRE, that is, alongside beauty, they should also inhold confidence and smartness.
The competition begins right from Casting where contestants' details are validated.
Thereafter, successful contestants go through to the Preliminary Voting round, here Fans decide who stays and who goes as not everyone casted makes it, only those with the mandated number of votes would.
Successful contestants are then referred to as Finalists, they get invited for an "Audition Proper" where they have a private one-to-one interaction with the Judges who now seem friendlier and more accommodating as the Finalists are just a few steps from joining the Miss Universe Anambra Family.
The judges give them advices and secrets on winning the competition as well as answer questions they may have and partially welcomes each into the big family with a letter.
The Final Voting exercise commences afterwards. At this point, finalists compete to secure their slot in Top Ten(10). This is the "Viewers' Choice Top Ten" search which ends on the day of Finale.
Next is Camp, here the finalists are expected to live their normal lives whilst comporting themselves with grace under pressure. They are advised to put in their very best in all camp activities as the whole Organisation would be watching and taking cognizance of everything. Camp is the driving force in selecting potential titleholders.
The Finale kicks off with an introduction of the finalists. Each finalist walks the stage during the bikini or swimsuit segment conspicuously depicting her fervent affection for a healthy lifestyle. They subsequently move on to the Costume show segment where the Judges and Fans espy their personal panache.
The Evening gown segment is the final look. After this, the scores are sumed up and the Top 10 finalists emerge, bringing them to the Final Question round. Each finalist randomly chooses a Judge to present her with a question focused on manifold aspects affecting humanity. This helps determine their ability to eloquently enunciate themselves under pressure whilst sharing a thoughtful, precise and well-informed answer.
Finally, all scores are again sumed up and the host calls out the Top 3 finalists, then the second runner-up. With just two finalists left, it is made known that: "In the event the winner is unable to fulfill her duties as Queen, the first runner-up takes over". The event concludes with the host ultimately announcing the Winner and she taking her first walk as Miss Universe Anambra.
